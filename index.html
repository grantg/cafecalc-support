CafeCalc

The coffee ratio calculator for precise, repeatable brews — with custom recipes and brew logging built in.

✦ Features

  • Instant coffee-to-water ratio calculator for any brew size
  • Support for all common brew methods (pour over, French press, espresso, and more)
  • Create and save custom brewing recipes with your preferred ratios and parameters
  • Log your brews and rate each cup to track what works best
  • Clean, minimal interface designed for the kitchen counter
